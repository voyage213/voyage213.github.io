Kuo-Wei Lu呂國維

A master's student working at the meeting of postmodern society and generative AI — how AI rewrites language, memory, and human experience — and a longtime essayist bringing these ideas to the public.

Master's Thesis碩士論文

  1. Thesis

    Postmodern Society and Generative AI: ChatGPT’s Globalized Reverse Tower-of-Babel Project. Drawing on postmodern social theory, it examines how generative AI reorganizes cross-lingual communication and the production of meaning as a "reverse Tower-of-Babel project" — when machines translate instantly across languages, how are human linguistic difference, cultural boundaries, and the power relations of knowledge rewritten? Advisor: Yu-Cheng Liu.
