Global Benchmarks國際視野

In an AI-center landscape dominated by engineering and medicine, this Center fills a crucial gap: the social study of AI.

University-based AI research centers with cross-college, interdisciplinary, and outreach functions have leading examples in both the U.S. and Taiwan. USC’s Center for AI in Society (CAIS) — focused on AI for Public Policy, AI for Social Good, criminal justice, and social welfare — is closest to this Center’s "study AI through the social sciences" identity.

Leading U.S. University AI Centers

CenterUniversityFocus
Stanford HAI Stanford University Founded 2019; one of the world’s most influential AI centers. Human-centered AI: AI × law, education, medicine, public policy, and AI governance; partners include OpenAI, Google DeepMind, WHO, and OECD.
MIT CSAIL MIT One of the world’s largest AI research centers. Foundation models, robotics, computer vision, LLMs, and AI for Science.
BAIR UC Berkeley Machine learning, reinforcement learning, LLMs, robotics, and AI ethics, with over 300 researchers.
Kempner Institute Harvard University The meeting of natural and artificial intelligence: AI × neuroscience, foundation models, and AI biology.
ML@GT Georgia Tech AI, robotics, smart cities, and AI safety.
CAIS ★ USC Center for AI in Society — AI for Public Policy, AI for Social Good, criminal justice, and social welfare; the AI center closest to a social-science orientation.
Center for ML Carnegie Mellon NLP, AI safety, robotics, and reinforcement learning.
Michigan AI Lab University of Michigan Autonomous systems, computer vision, LLMs, and medical AI.

Leading Taiwan University AI Centers

UniversityAI centerFocus
NTU AI Center of Research Excellence (AI Core) A university-level flagship founded in 2025: trustworthy AI, explainable AI, foundation models, green AI, multilingual large models, and AI education and ethics; international partners include MIT, Stanford, and Google DeepMind.
NTU AINTU (Joint Research Center for AI Technology and All Vista Healthcare) Founded 2018. Medical AI, NLP, computer vision, and AI law and policy; a key site of the NSTC AI innovation research-center program.
NYCU PAIR (Pervasive AI Research Center) AI robotics, smart transportation, medical AI, and smart cities.
NTHU AIMS (AI Manufacturing System Research Center) Smart manufacturing, industrial AI, digital twins, and edge AI.
NCKU AI Biotechnology Research Center Biomedical AI, precision medicine, medical imaging, and drug development.
NYCU Wistron–NYCU Embedded AI Research Center An industry–academia center: embedded AI, edge AI, smart manufacturing, and smart transportation.

Positioned to "study AI through a sociological lens," this Center fills a rare but increasingly vital gap in Taiwan’s higher education, amid the engineering- and medicine-dominated landscape above: the social study of AI.
